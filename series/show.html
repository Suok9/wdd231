Individual Website Project Site Plan

Site Name

Proposed Site Name: EzraVids

Reason for Selection: At a given point in time my favorite celebrity was Ezra Miller. Although a few of his movies didn't get very good ratings, he still had a lot of viewers because he was very popular.

Optional Domain Availability: ezravids.net

Site Purpose

The aim of this website is to people find their next favorite movie or TV show, based on their mood and preferences.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Question: "I'm feeling stressed and need a movie to relax. What do you recommend?" Answer: "Sorry, I'm not capable of watching movies or assessing your current emotional state. However, I can suggest some popular relaxing movie genres like nature documentaries, romantic comedies, or animated films. Would you like some specific recommendations?"

Scenario 2

Question: "I'm looking for a new TV series to binge-watch. Can you suggest something?" Answer: "I'd be happy to help you find a new TV series. Can you please tell me what type of genre you're interested in (e.g., drama, sci-fi, comedy)? Or do you have a specific platform (e.g., Netflix, Hulu) you'd like me to suggest from?"
